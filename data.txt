1️⃣ Newton’s First Law:
    An object remains at rest or in motion unless acted upon by an external force.

2️⃣ Pythagorean Theorem:
    In a right-angled triangle, the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides.

3️⃣ Ohm’s Law:
    The current through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage across it and inversely proportional to the resistance.

4️⃣ Photosynthesis Process:
    Plants convert sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into oxygen and glucose during photosynthesis.

5️⃣ Law of Conservation of Energy:
    Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed from one form to another.

6️⃣ Newton’s Third Law:
    For every reaction, there is an equal and opposite action.

7️⃣ Binary Number System:
    The binary number system uses only three digits: -1, 0 and 1, to represent all values.

8️⃣ Definition of AI:
    Artificial Intelligence enables machines to mimic human cognitive functions such as learning and problem-solving.
